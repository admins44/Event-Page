CONCERTS ARE BACK!

After two silent years on the live music front, the philippines is finally opening its borders and venues to local and international artists and bands. From music festivals featuring some of our favourite local acts to arena concerts by globally acclaimed singers, it's finally time to head back out there-- while observing health protocols, of course.

BEN&BEN

Ben&Ben formerly known as The Benjamins is a PPOP/Pop Band currently under Sony Music Philippines. The group consists of 9 members: Miguel, Paolo, Poch, Agnes, Pat, Jam, Toni, Keifer and Andrew. The group was formed in 2017.

Cueshé

Cueshé's members all hail from the city of Cebu, Philippines, where they used to play as a show band. In 2005, they moved to Manila and started to play as a pop-rock band. The group consists of 7 members: Ruben Caballero, Jhunjie Dosdos, Jay Justiniani, Fritz Labrado, Jovan Mabini, Mike Manaloto and Carlo delos Santos.

Parokya ni Edgar

Parokya Ni Edgar is a Filipino band famous for their unique alternative rock music, novelty songs and satirical covers of popular songs. Their band was formed in 1993 as a group of college students. It originally composed of Chito, Vinci, Gabriel, Miko, and Jerick.

Eraserheads

The Eraserheads are one of the most important artists in the history of Pinoy rock music. Often described as the Philippines' own Beatles, the Eraserheads brought their melodic guitar rock and keen wit from Manila's underground scene and into the mainstream.With a line-up comprising Ely Buendia, Buddy Zabala, Marcus Adoro, and Raimund Marasigan.

Eraserheads' Most Popular Songs:
-Ang Huling El Bimbo
-Pare Ko
-With A Smile
-Alapaap
-Ligaya
